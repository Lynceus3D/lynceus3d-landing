Why Lynceus3D Pilot?

Drone footage was always meant to be 3D — but the tools to do it properly didn’t exist. Traditional 3D workflows are inconsistent, uncomfortable, and too technical for most people to use. It’s easy to end up with leaning trees, distorted buildings, and 3D that feels “off”.

Lynceus3D Pilot™ fixes that automatically. The system analyzes your footage and produces stable, natural-looking depth so your 3D feels cinematic instead of experimental.

Autopilot™

Your intelligent 3D engine.

  • Automatically balances 3D depth for a comfortable viewing experience.
  • Keeps verticals (trees, towers, buildings) standing upright instead of “leaning”.
  • Smooths depth transitions across the entire flight — orbits, fly-bys, climbs, and descents.
  • Reduces the guesswork of stereo tuning down to a single click.

Autopilot turns raw footage into consistent, watchable 3D without forcing you to become a stereo engineer.

FlightView™

Your final-check 3D preview surface.

  • Clean side-by-side playback optimized for YouTube, Viture, Quest, Pico, and other XR headsets.
  • Lets you visually confirm depth, comfort, and geometry before you publish.
  • Makes it easy to review and share 3D results without touching your editing timeline.
  • Designed for quick yes/no confidence checks on every shot.

FlightView shows you how your 3D will actually look on real devices, not just in theory.

What Proper 3D Is Supposed to Look Like

  • Upright trees, towers, and buildings instead of leaning toward or away from the viewer.
  • Comfortable depth that feels natural to watch, not like a gimmick.
  • Smooth 3D during orbits, turns, and direction changes — without sudden depth jumps.
  • Stable stereo in forests, towns, coastlines, and open landscapes.
  • A polished, “miniature diorama” feel when the scene supports it.

With Lynceus3D Pilot™, you’ll know immediately when you see it — your footage stops looking like an experiment and starts looking like finished 3D.

Who It’s For

  • Drone pilots & filmmakers – turn everyday flights into immersive 3D sequences.
  • VR / XR viewers (Viture, Quest, Pico) – finally get clean, properly formatted SBS content.
  • 3D enthusiasts – enjoy consistent, stable stereo without manual tweaking.
  • Technical professionals – anyone who needs reliable spatial accuracy and clean depth presentation.
